Thermodynamics

The study of energy and how it moves.
The physics of heat, work, and temperature

State Functions

Formula: A = U - TS

Units: all units of energy (J, kJ, etc)
Description: Helmholtz free energy is similar to Gibbs free energy but applies to systems at constant volume and temperature. It represents the maximum amount of useful work that can be extracted from a system at constant volume and temperature.
Uses: Used to evaluate the math for constant pressure systems. It combines multiple, solvable properties into a workable variable.
