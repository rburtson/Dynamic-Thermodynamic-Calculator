Thermodynamics

The study of energy and how it moves.
The physics of heat, work, and temperature

State Functions

Formula: PV = nRT

Units: Pascals, Bar, ATM
Description: Pressure is the force exerted per unit area and is typically measured in units such as Pascals (Pa) or atmospheres (atm). In a gas, pressure is caused by the collisions of gas molecules with the walls of their container. In a fluid, pressure arises from the weight of the fluid above a given point.
