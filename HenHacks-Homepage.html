Thermodynamics

The study of energy and how it moves.
The physics of heat, work, and temperature

The Dynamic Thermodynamic Calculator

By: Ryan Burtson (rburtson@udel.edu), Jackson Myers (jmyrs@udel.edu), Collin Owens (crow@udel.edu)

Understanding the relationships between thermodynamic properties can be difficult. The objective of this project is to help a newcomer to thermodynamics make and visualize the initial connections that are needed to succeed.

Shown above is a diagram of the Carnot Engine: TH represents the temperature of the hot body, QH, the heat flow from the hot body, QC, the heat flow to the cold body, TC, the temperature of the cold body, and W represents the useful energy extracted from the process.
